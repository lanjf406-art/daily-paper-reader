## 摘要
引言：靶向Nectin-4的抗体-药物偶联物（ADC）恩诺单抗（EV）联合帕博利珠单抗是局部晚期或转移性尿路上皮癌（UC）患者的一线治疗方案。对于EV联合帕博利珠单抗无应答或进展的患者，最佳治疗策略仍是未满足的临床需求。我们试图描述ADC和免疫治疗（IO）相关靶点表达谱，以识别EV之外的可选治疗脆弱性。

方法：我们通过文献综述识别在UC中具有已批准或研究相关性的ADC和IO靶点。使用无监督层次聚类在RNA-seq数据中识别靶基因表达聚类。对来自癌症基因组图谱膀胱尿路上皮癌队列（TCGA-BLCA）的434名患者进行转录组聚类分析，并在来自肿瘤研究信息交换网络（ORIEN）联盟的独立队列（478名患者）中进行验证。使用来自额外116名患者队列的质谱数据对这些靶点进行蛋白质组学检测。差异基因表达分析评估靶点表达模式、组织学变异和肌层浸润性膀胱癌共识分子亚型（CMIBC）之间的关联。

结果：我们识别了13个ADC和10个IO相关靶点，在UC中具有转化相关性。转录组分析揭示了三个可重复的过表达靶基因聚类，跨独立队列一致：1）腔面/上皮相关聚类，富集VTCN1、SLITRK6、FGFR3、NECTIN4、TACSTD2、ERBB2和ERBB3；2）免疫靶点主导聚类，富集BTLA、LAG3、PDCD1、TIGIT、CTLA4、TNFRSF9、TNFRSF18、TNFRSF4；3）基底/神经内分泌相关聚类，以CD274、F3、NT5E、EGFR、MET和DLL3为特征。类似聚类在蛋白质组水平上基本保守。腺癌中ERBB3过表达高于神经内分泌癌和鳞状细胞癌。纯鳞状细胞癌中TACSTD2过表达高于腺癌。在CMIBC亚型中，基底/鳞状肿瘤表达更高水平的CD274、EGFR、F3、LAG3、NT5E和TNFRSF18，而腔面肿瘤显示更高的ERBB2和ERBB3表达。神经内分泌样肿瘤相比所有其他亚型显示更高的DLL3表达。NECTIN4、TACSTD2和FGFR3低表达的肿瘤富含替代靶点，包括DLL3、CD274和CD276。我们的发现为晚期UC中基于假说驱动的治疗优先排序提供了框架。

结论：UC以可重复的、生物学上不同的ADC和IO靶点表达模式为特征。NECTIN4的表达水平与TACSTD2、FGFR3正相关，与DLL3、CD276和CD274负相关，支持EV之外的替代性生物学知情治疗策略。UC的组织学变异和分子亚型也显示出不同的靶点表达模式。本研究首次提供了整合性转录组框架，将ADC和IO靶点共表达模式联系起来，用于假说驱动治疗优先排序。这些发现为晚期UC中合理的ADC和免疫治疗开发提供了基础，并支持在按治疗分层的队列中进行前瞻性蛋白质组学验证。

转化相关性声明：恩诺单抗联合帕博利珠单抗重新定义了晚期尿路上皮癌的一线治疗，但耐药或进展后的治疗选择仍未明确。在本研究中，我们跨独立队列整合转录组和蛋白质组分析，以定义尿路上皮癌中抗体-药物偶联物（ADC）和免疫治疗靶点共表达的可重复模式。我们识别出与组织学和分子亚型相关的生物学上不同的靶点表达模式，并展示了治疗可操作靶点之间的协调关系，在某些情况下是互斥关系。

这些发现具有直接的转化意义。首先，它们为基于NECTIN4富集肿瘤中共表达靶点的合理序贯和联合策略提供了生物学依据。其次，它们在NECTIN4低表达的肿瘤中识别了替代治疗脆弱性，包括DLL3和CD274相关通路，这些肿瘤可能对EV为基础的治疗耐药。最后，该框架为尿路上皮癌中生物标志物驱动的临床试验奠定了基础，并支持超越当前标准的精准治疗方法的发展。

## Abstract
IntroductionNectin-4 targeting antibody-drug conjugate (ADC) enfortumab vedotin (EV), in combination with pembrolizumab, is the first-line treatment for patients with locally advanced or metastatic urothelial carcinoma (UC). Optimal treatment strategies for patients who are non-responders or progress on EV with pembrolizumab remain an unmet clinical need. We sought to characterize ADC and immunotherapy (IO)-associated target expression profiles to identify candidate therapeutic vulnerabilities beyond EV.

MethodsWe conducted a literature review to identify ADC and IO targets with approved or investigational relevance in UC. Unsupervised hierarchical clustering was used to identify clusters of target gene expression in RNA-seq data. Transcriptomic clustering analyses were performed in 434 patients from The Cancer Genome Atlas Bladder Urothelial Carcinoma cohort (TCGA-BLCA) and validated in an independent cohort of 478 patients from the Oncology Research Information Exchange Network (ORIEN) consortium. Proteomic interrogation of these targets was performed using mass spectrometry data from additional cohort of 116 patients. Differential gene expression analyses evaluated associations between target expression patterns, histologic variants, and consensus molecular subtypes of muscle-invasive bladder cancer (CMIBC).

ResultsWe identified 13 ADC and 10 IO-associated targets with translational relevance in UC. Transcriptomic analyses revealed three reproducible clusters of overexpressed target genes across independent cohorts: 1) a luminal/epithelial-associated cluster enriched for VTCN1, SLITRK6, FGFR3, NECTIN4, TACSTD2, ERBB2, and ERBB3; 2) an immune target predominant cluster enriched for BTLA, LAG3, PDCD1, TIGIT, CTLA4, TNFRSF9, TNFRSF18, TNFRSF4; and 3) a basal/neuroendocrine-associated cluster characterized by CD274, F3, NT5E, EGFR, MET and DLL3. Similar clusters were largely conserved at the proteomic level. Adenocarcinomas overexpressed ERBB3 compared to neuroendocrine and squamous cell carcinomas. Pure squamous cell carcinomas overexpressed TACSTD2 compared to adenocarcinomas. In CMIBC subtypes, basal/squamous tumors expressed higher levels of CD274, EGFR, F3, LAG3, NT5E, and TNFRSF18, whereas luminal tumors demonstrated higher ERBB2 and ERBB3 expression. Neuroendocrine-like tumors showed higher DLL3 expression compared to all other subtypes. Tumors with low expression of NECTIN4, TACSTD2, and FGFR3 were enriched for alternative targets including DLL3, CD274, and CD276. Our findings provide a framework for hypothesis-driven therapeutic prioritization in advanced UC.

Conclusions:

UC is characterized by reproducible, biologically distinct patterns of ADC and IO target expressions. The degree of expression of NECTIN4 was positively associated with TACSTD2, FGFR3 and inversely associated with DLL3, CD276, and CD274, supporting alternative biologically informed treatment strategies besides EV . Histologic variants and molecular subtypes of UC also display distinct patterns of target expression. This study provides the first integrated transcriptomic framework linking ADC and IO target co-expression patterns for hypothesis-driven therapeutic prioritization. These findings provide a basis for rational ADC and immunotherapy development in advanced UC and support prospective proteomic validation in treatment stratified cohorts.

Statement of Translational RelevanceEnfortumab vedotin plus pembrolizumab has redefined first-line therapy for advanced urothelial carcinoma, yet treatment selection following resistance or progression remains undefined. In this study, we integrate transcriptomic and proteomic analyses across independent cohorts to define reproducible patterns of antibody-drug conjugate (ADC) and immunotherapy target co-expression in urothelial carcinoma. We identify biologically distinct target-expression patterns that are associated with histologic and molecular subtypes and demonstrate coordinated and, in some cases, mutually exclusive relationships among therapeutically actionable targets.

These findings have direct translational implications. First, they provide biologic rationale for rational sequencing and combination strategies based on co-expressed targets in NECTIN4-enriched tumors. Second, they identify alternative therapeutic vulnerabilities, including DLL3- and CD274-associated pathways, in tumors with low NECTIN4 expression, a population potentially enriched for resistance to EV-based therapy. Finally, this framework establishes a foundation for biomarker-driven clinical trials in urothelial carcinoma and supports the development of precision therapeutic approaches beyond current standards.

    - 缺乏前瞻性治疗分配或响应数据，所有临床效用均为假说。

### 6. 论文的主要结论与发现

- **三个可重复的靶点共表达簇**：
  - **Cluster 1（腔面/上皮ADC簇）**：VTCN1、SLITRK6、FGFR3、NECTIN4、TACSTD2、ERBB2、ERBB3 → 适合联合靶向Nectin-4 + FGFR3/HER2/TROP2等。
  - **Cluster 2（免疫检查点簇）**：BTLA、LAG3、PDCD1、TIGIT、CTLA4、TNFRSF9、TNFRSF18、TNFRSF4 → 可能受益于联合IO策略（如PD-1+LAG-3等）。
  - **Cluster 3（基底/神经内分泌簇）**：CD274、F3、NT5E、EGFR、MET、DLL3 → 对应替代靶点如DLL3（神经内分泌样）、EGFR/MET、PD-L1。
- **NECTIN4表达与Cluster 1靶点正相关，与Cluster 3靶点（DLL3、CD274、CD276）负相关** → 支持“NECTIN4低表达肿瘤可转向DLL3/PD-L1靶向”的假设。
- **组织学/分子亚型特异性**：
  - 鳞癌：高表达EGFR、F3、NECTIN4、TACSTD2。
  - 腺癌：高表达ERBB3。
  - 神经内分泌样肿瘤：高表达DLL3。
  - 基底/鳞状分子亚型：高表达CD274、EGFR、LAG3等。
  - 腔面分子亚型：高表达ERBB2、ERBB3。
- **临床案例**：1例神经内分泌UC患者对DLL3双特异性抗体tarlatamab有效；1例EV+SG后仍共表达NECTIN4/FGFR3/HER2/TROP2。

### 7. 优点

- **多队列、跨平台验证**：TCGA和ORIEN两个大样本独立队列的转录组结果高度一致，且部分得到蛋白组数据支持，使靶点共表达模式具有生物学稳健性。
- **系统整合**：首次系统地将ADC靶点与IO靶点放在同一框架内分析，揭示了它们之间的共表达或互斥关系，而非孤立研究单一靶点。
- **临床关联性强**：将靶点模式与病理医生报告的组织学变异和共识分子亚型关联，有助于在日常病理报告有限的背景下进行生物学推断。
- **提出可检验的假说**：明确提出了基于NECTIN4高低表达进行替代或联合治疗策略的框架，为后续临床试验设计提供具体方向。
- **忠实报告局限性**：作者明确指出了预印本性质、缺乏前瞻性验证、生存分析不显著等局限，没有过度夸大结论。

### 8. 不足与局限

- **缺乏直接预测证据**：所有数据均来自前EV+帕博利珠单抗时代的标本，无法直接评估靶点表达对当前治疗的预测价值。靶点共表达是否真的对应治疗响应尚需前瞻性试验。
- **蛋白质组覆盖不足**：23个靶点中仅13个可被定量，NECTIN4蛋白数据缺失，降低了转录组-蛋白组验证的完整性。
- **组织学样本不平衡**：腺癌和神经内分泌癌样本量极小，差异分析统计效力低，影响结论外推性。
- **无对比方法**：未与任何现有预测模型（如基于突变的分类、单一标志物IHC方法）进行对比，不清楚该转录组框架相比单纯检测NECTIN4是否带来额外的临床价值。
- **生存分析阴性**：靶点聚类与总生存无显著关联，提示这些表达模式可能反映肿瘤固有生物学而非预后因素，这对长期终点设计的临床试验是挑战。
- **计算细节缺失**：未提供层次聚类的稳定性评估（如bootstrapping），也没有报告不同聚类数目的客观准则（如elbow plot），聚类数(k=3)的选取理由不够充分。
- **临床案例仅为例证**：两个病例为描述性，不能作为疗效证据，存在选择偏倚。
