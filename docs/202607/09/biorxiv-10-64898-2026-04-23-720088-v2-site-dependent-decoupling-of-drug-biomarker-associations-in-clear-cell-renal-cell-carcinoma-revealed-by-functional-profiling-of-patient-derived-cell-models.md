## 摘要
透明细胞肾细胞癌（ccRCC）常对标准治疗方案表现出原发性和获得性耐药，凸显了改进治疗反应预测策略和优先考虑患者特异性治疗的必要性。尽管近期的多组学和单细胞研究揭示了ccRCC的分子景观，但仅凭分子改变并不能完全预测药物敏感性。我们前瞻性地分析了28例局限性和转移性ccRCC患者的肿瘤，整合了分子特征、患者来源细胞模型中的功能性药物敏感性和耐药性检测，以及纵向临床数据。尽管基因组生物标志物提示27/28例患者存在潜在可靶向治疗，但功能检测揭示了基因组可操作性与离体药物敏感性之间的不一致，而线性混合效应模型发现了16种与11种药物敏感性相关的新型拷贝数特征。基因型-药物反应关联在原发肿瘤和腔静脉血栓中基本保留，但在远处转移中经常被破坏。将功能性药物检测与多组学分析相结合，揭示了仅凭基因组数据无法显现的脆弱性，优化了治疗可操作性，捕捉了ccRCC患者间和不同部位的异质性，并为个体化治疗优先排序提供了可扩展的框架。

## Abstract
Clear cell renal cell carcinoma (ccRCC) frequently exhibits primary and acquired resistance to standard-of-care therapies, underscoring the need for improved strategies to predict therapeutic response and prioritize patient-specific treatments. Although recent multi-omic and single-cell studies have provided insight into the molecular landscape of ccRCC, molecular alterations alone incompletely predict drug sensitivity. We prospectively profiled tumors from 28 patients with localized and metastatic ccRCC by integrating molecular characterization, functional drug sensitivity and resistance testing in patient-derived cell models, and longitudinal clinical data. Although genomic biomarkers suggested potentially actionable therapies in 27/28 patients, functional testing revealed discordance between genomic actionability and ex vivo drug sensitivity, whereas linear mixed-effects modelling uncovered 16 novel copy-number-based features associated with sensitivity to 11 drugs. Genotype-drug response associations were largely preserved between primary tumors and vena cava thrombi but frequently disrupted in distant metastases. Integrating functional drug testing with multi-omic profiling reveals vulnerabilities not apparent from genomic data alone, refines therapeutic actionability, captures interpatient and intersite heterogeneity in ccRCC, and provides a scalable framework for individualized treatment prioritization.

- **背景**：透明细胞肾细胞癌（ccRCC）常对标准治疗（如TKI、免疫检查点抑制剂、HIF-2α抑制剂）产生原发或获得性耐药。尽管多组学和单细胞研究描绘了ccRCC的分子图谱，但仅凭分子改变无法充分预测治疗反应。
- **核心问题**：如何整合基因组信息与功能性药物敏感性数据，识别更准确的预测性生物标志物，并理解不同解剖部位（原发灶、腔静脉癌栓、远处转移）之间药物-标志物关联的稳定性。
