## 摘要
对循环肿瘤DNA进行全外显子测序能够纵向监测肿瘤动态、演变和治疗反应，但在低输入、低脱落量的情况下（如胰腺导管腺癌）仍面临技术挑战。本研究系统比较了三种市售低输入全外显子测序流程，这些流程采用安捷伦和凯杰的外显子捕获设计，并使用从胰腺导管腺癌患者多个匹配纵向血浆样本中提取的超低输入cfDNA。

通过使用预定义的性能指标（包括覆盖度、重复率和变异检测）以及患者临床基因组分析相关的其他指标，我们展示了所有三种流程均能产生高质量测序数据，即使是在非常低输入的cfDNA条件下。在本研究测试的条件下，安捷伦V8流程在低输入、低肿瘤分数cfDNA的全外显子测序中，在覆盖均匀性、测序效率和热点覆盖方面提供了最有利的平衡。

这些发现表明，包括捕获区域在内的流程设计显著影响低输入临床环境下的ctDNA全外显子测序性能。这些发现对于早期和/或微小残留病灶的情况尤为重要，因为此时肿瘤分数较低，从有限输入样本中恢复基因组信息至关重要。

## Abstract
Whole exome sequencing (WES) of circulating tumour DNA (ctDNA) enables longitudinal monitoring of tumour dynamics, evolution and treatment response but remains technically challenging in low-input, low-shedding settings such as pancreatic ductal adenocarcinoma (PDAC). Here, we systematically compared three commercially available low-input WES workflows incorporating Agilent (V6, V8) and Qiagen exome capture designs using ultra-low input cfDNAs extracted from multiple matched longitudinal plasma samples from PDAC patients.

Using predefined performance metrics including coverage, duplication rate and variant detection and additional metrics relevant for clinical genomic profiling in patient care, we show that all three workflows produced high-quality sequencing data, even from very low input cfDNA. Within the conditions tested here, the Agilent V8 workflow provided the most favourable balance of coverage uniformity, sequencing efficiency and hotspot coverage for low input, low tumour fraction cfDNA WES.

These findings demonstrate that workflow design, including capture footprint, substantially influences ctDNA WES performance in low-input clinical contexts. These findings are particularly relevant in early stage and/or minimal residual disease settings, where tumour fractions are low and recovery of genomic information from limited-input samples is critical.
