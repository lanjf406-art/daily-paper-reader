## 摘要
177Lu-PSMA-617（PluvictoTM，Lu-177 RLT）是一种经FDA批准的靶向放射配体疗法（RLT），用于转移性去势抵抗性前列腺癌（mCRPC），但这种单一方法的应答持久性对该领域构成挑战。嵌合抗原受体（CAR）T细胞疗法已彻底改变了血液恶性肿瘤的临床实践，但其在包括mCRPC在内的实体瘤中的临床开发受到抗原异质性和免疫抑制性肿瘤微环境（TME）的阻碍。在本研究中，我们评估了Lu-177 RLT与PSCA-CAR T细胞的治疗组合以克服这些障碍。在具有同质或异质抗原表达的人异种移植和小鼠同基因前列腺癌模型中，与单一疗法相比，序贯给予Lu-177 RLT、环磷酰胺（Cy）和PSCA-CAR T细胞可改善肿瘤控制并延长生存期。机制上，Lu-177 RLT单独或联合Cy通过促进促炎性髓系反应和激活内源性T细胞来重塑TME，同时增强CAR T细胞的激活和效应功能。我们还评估了225Ac-PSMA-617 RLT作为与CAR T细胞联合的新兴方法，并观察到抗肿瘤反应，支持其作为替代RLT伙伴的潜力。这些发现支持RLT作为一种免疫启动策略来增强CAR T细胞疗法，并为该组合在mCRPC中的临床转化提供了理论基础。

## Abstract
177Lu-PSMA-617 (PluvictoTM, Lu-177 RLT) is an FDA-approved targeted radioligand therapy (RLT) for metastatic castration-resistant prostate cancer (mCRPC), but its durability of response to this singular approach poses a challenge to the field. Chimeric antigen receptor (CAR) T cell therapy has revolutionized clinical practice for hematological malignancies, but its clinical development for solid tumors, including mCRPC, has been encumbered by antigen heterogeneity and the immunosuppressive tumor microenvironment (TME). Here, we evaluate the therapeutic combination of Lu-177 RLT and PSCA-CAR T cells to overcome these barriers. In human xenograft and mouse syngeneic prostate cancer models with homogeneous or heterogeneous antigen expression, the sequential administration of Lu-177 RLT, cyclophosphamide (Cy), and PSCA-CAR T cells improves tumor control and prolongs survival compared to monotherapies. Mechanistically, Lu-177 RLT alone or with Cy remodels the TME by promoting pro-inflammatory myeloid responses and activating endogenous T cells, while enhancing CAR T cell activation and effector function. We additionally evaluated 225Ac-PSMA-617 RLT as an emerging approach in combination with CAR T cells and observed anti-tumor responses, supporting its potential as an alternative RLT partner. These findings support RLT as an immune priming strategy to enhance CAR T cell therapy and provide a rationale for clinical translation of this combination in mCRPC.

## 6. 论文的主要结论与发现

1. **PSMA和PSCA在人类前列腺癌中高度异质表达**（PDX和HuPSA数据支持），为双靶向策略提供依据。
2. **177Lu RLT + PSCA-CAR T细胞序贯治疗在异种移植模型中显著延长生存**（40%长期治好），且耐受良好（无血液学毒性）。
3. **在同源免疫活性模型中**：
   - RLT联合Cy重塑TME：增加M1样巨噬细胞MHC I/II类抗原呈递、趋化因子信号；减少T细胞耗竭标志物；促进内源性T细胞向效应/记忆表型转化。
   - 在抗原异质肿瘤中，RLT + Cy + PSCA-CAR相比于Cy + PSCA-CAR或RLT + Cy + 非靶向CAR显著提高治好率和生存期（P=0.0414），疗效与双CAR对照相当。
   - 治好小鼠可形成免疫记忆（再挑战后肿瘤生长延迟）。
   - **复发肿瘤分析**：早期复发多因PSCA⁻细胞逃逸（PSMA⁺）；晚期复发可同时丢失两种抗原。
4. **机制**：scRNA-seq显示RLT增强CAR T细胞激活（Tnfrsf9、Il2ra）、效应功能（Ifng）和干细胞性（Tcf7）；M1样巨噬细胞与CAR T细胞间MHC-II、MIF、ADGRL信号通路增强，提示髓系-T细胞互作。
5. **225Ac RLT + CAR T细胞组合**同样增强抗肿瘤活性，但伴有一定体重减轻（需优化Cy剂量）。
