## 摘要
治疗抵抗和缺乏明确的预测性生物标志物严重阻碍了免疫治疗在食管鳞癌（ESCC）中的临床推广。在此，我们确定葡萄糖转运蛋白1（GLUT1）是免疫治疗抵抗的关键决定因素。GLUT1表达升高与ESCC患者免疫治疗反应差及预后不良相关。GLUT1缺失或抑制可增强CD8 T细胞浸润和细胞毒性，并使ESCC肿瘤对抗PD-1（-PD1）治疗敏感。重要的是，饮食葡萄糖限制与-PD1联合使用时，表现出与GLUT1抑制相当的抗肿瘤疗效。机制上，GLUT1与HAT1和FOXM1建立正反馈回路，表观遗传重塑染色质可及性以抑制肿瘤细胞衰老，从而阻碍CD8 T细胞介导的抗肿瘤免疫。我们的发现强调了GLUT1作为免疫治疗抵抗的预测性生物标志物，并提示饮食葡萄糖限制是增强ESCC免疫治疗疗效的可行策略。

## Abstract
Therapeutic resistance and undefined predictive biomarkers severely hinder the clinical popularization of immunotherapy in esophageal squamous cell carcinoma (ESCC). Herein, we identify the glucose transporter 1 (GLUT1) as a critical determinant of immunotherapy resistance. Elevated expression of GLUT1 correlates with poor immunotherapy response and unfavorable prognosis in ESCC patients. GLUT1 deletion or inhibition enhances CD8 T cell infiltration and cytotoxicity, and sensitizes ESCC tumors to anti-PD-1 (-PD1) therapy. Importantly, dietary glucose restriction exhibits equivalent antitumor efficacy to GLUT1 inhibition when combined with -PD1. Mechanistically, GLUT1 establishes a positive feedback loop with HAT1 and FOXM1, which epigenetically remodels chromatin accessibility to suppress tumor cell senescence, thereby impeding CD8 T cell-mediated antitumor immunity. Our findings highlight GLUT1 as a predictive biomarker of immunotherapy resistance and suggest dietary glucose restriction as a viable strategy to potentiate immunotherapy efficacy in ESCC.

- **临床验证规模有限**：免疫化疗耐药验证仅25例患者，GLUT1作为预测标志物的普适性需要大型多中心队列确认。
